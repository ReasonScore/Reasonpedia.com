Fight Misinformation

Apply to be a part time researcher and fact checker.

Warning:

Please be sure this sounds like a task you would enjoy. Many personality types find it draining and burn out. This is probably good for people that like solving logic puzzles and detailed work with lots of reading and tedious data entry. A person with the right personality type could find it fulfilling.

If I get over 20 applications I may not be able to respond to everyone. If I do respond, it should be within a week of submission..

Requirements

  • Attention to detail
  • Consistent communication
  • Comfortable in published video calls with their camera on. We “Build in public”.
  • Intuitive understanding of United States common culture
  • Familiarity with reading scientific papers and scholarly articles
  • Ability to re-write statements into logical forms
  • Data entry into complex data formats
  • Ability to strengthen arguments you disagree with
  • 10-20 hours a week
  • 2 weekly 2 hour working meetings at consistent times.

Tasks

  • Participate in public video meeting with your camera on
  • Find common well worded public arguments on all sides of assigned topics
  • Research the evidence and logic behind all sides of public arguments
  • Organize and re-write the arguments in a specific logical form while reducing sensationalism
